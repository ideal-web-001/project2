MS-DOS: -

MS-DOS stands for Microsoft disk operating system. It is a single operating system. For IBM and IBM compatible personal. It was introduced in 1981 jointly Microsoft and IBM and was the most popular operating system for personal computer. In the 1980s because of its popularity, Microsoft took a decision to launch independently windows O.S In 1990s.
